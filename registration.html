Step 6: Parent / Guardian Agreement

I, the parent/guardian of the above named child, give my permission and approval for his/her participation in all activities during the football season. I do hereby waive, release, absolve and indemnify and agree to hold harmless local football league organizers, sponsors, supervisors, coaches, participants and persons transporting my child to and from games, practices, etc. for any claim arising out of injury to my child whether the result of negligence or any other cause during practices, games or during transportation.

We, the parents/guardians also agree to return the uniform and/or equipment issued in the same condition as received, with the exception of wear experienced through normal use. If the uniform is not returned, I understand that I must reimburse the league $250.00 for the cost of the uniform.

The insurance provided through Bath County Jr Pro is paid for out of the funds raised. The Bath County Jr Pro will not pay the deductible. It is a secondary policy when you have other medical and accidental insurance, but it is the primary coverage if the above named child is not otherwise insured.

You should be aware that in spite of all reasonable precautions, injuries could happen. Football is a collision sport, and even the best equipment will sometimes not prevent injury.
